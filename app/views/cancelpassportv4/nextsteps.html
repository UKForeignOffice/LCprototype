Confirmation and next steps

Your application has been submitted.

You've chosen to pay at the consulate. Bring your credit or debit card with you for your appointment. If you wish to pay in cash, consulates accept local currency rather than British pounds.

You must attend the British Consulate General Amsterdam in person to complete your application. Click Next below to book your appointment.

Warning You'll need to bring with you:

  • proof of your travel plans, eg flight booking confirmations (or detailed written plans if you can't book ahead)
  • a local police report if your passport has been stolen or your current passport if you still have it
  • proof of residency if your final destination is outside of the UK or the European Economic Area
  • a passport-sized photograph (passport photo requirements)
